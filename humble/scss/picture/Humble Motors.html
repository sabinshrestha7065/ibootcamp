Part 1 - Harness the Sun's Power

We believe the future of global transportation is solar powered.
By integrating panels into an EV, we can harness the power of the sun while we drive. Today, automobiles are still too dependant on nonrenewable energy; even electric vehicles rely on America's energy grid, 70% of which is powered by fossil fuels. Instead, by integrating solar technology into an electric vehicle, we can generate the clean energy we need directly into our cars.

Part 2 - Humble Harnesses the Sun

The Humble One has 80 square
feet of revolutionary, solar cells that can generate over three megawatt-hours
of power in a single year. The cells are connected to an onboard photovoltaic system of panels, chargers, and converters that power the vehicle's battery, allowing travel without the need to stop and charge. These innovations represent a first step towards a brighter solar electric future.

Up to 35 Extra Miles Per Day

One of the biggest benefits
of integrating Humble solar technology into an existing electric vehicle is increased range. Depending on where
you live, you can add an extra 15+ miles everyday without
the need for a charge station.

Part 3 - Designing the Humble One

Our design for the Humble One stems from a desire to create something that is fun to drive and safe for travel, while surrounding the driver with exceptional craftsmanship. Combining clever uses of strong yet light materials like aluminum, high strength steel and carbon fiber have cut the Humble One's body and chassis weight in half.
A lighter solar vehicle means an increase in overall range and more freedom.

WITH A MORE LIGHTWEIGHT PLATFORM,
THE MORE EFFICIENT OUR SOLAR POWER BECOMES

Lightweight and Efficient

With a lightweight but rigid platform, the Humble One
has been designed with sharp handling in mind. Structural adhesives and laser screw welds significantly reduce the need for heavier screws and fasteners, enhancing agility
and ride quality.

A Solar Crossover SUV

An EV industry game-changer, the Humble One is
the world’s first solar crossover electric vehicle to pair a potent onboard solar panel system with a powerful electric motor and a self-charging battery.

Part 4 - The Benefits of Solar

Humble One has been engineered from a radically different perspective.
We threw out conventional thinking and guided only by the laws of physics, sought to get the most out of every joule of energy from the sun. The result will be the world’s most sustainable vehicle thanks in part to a full-length solar roof, semi-transparent solar panel windows, and a state of the art 80kWh battery.

Thousands of Yearly Miles

The Humble One will provide up to 7,000 miles of future-proof power every year, depending on your location. And when the sun isn't shining, an impressive battery allows for a 250-300 mile range to keep you moving.
